The Front-End (Client-Side)
The front-end is the visual interface of a website or app. It encompasses everything the user physically sees and clicks on, including layouts, colors, text, images, and navigation menus
The Back-End (Server-Side)
The back-end is the engine that powers the front-end. It runs behind the scenes on a server and is responsible for managing data, processing requests, and ensuring everything operates smoothly
